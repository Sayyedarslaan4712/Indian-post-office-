About India Post

Trusted postal services since 1854

Connecting India for Over a Century

India Post has been serving the nation since 1854, providing reliable postal services to millions of customers across the country. With a rich heritage and commitment to excellence, we continue to evolve to meet the changing needs of our customers.

As a key part of India's communication infrastructure, we pride ourselves on our extensive network that reaches even the most remote areas of the country.
